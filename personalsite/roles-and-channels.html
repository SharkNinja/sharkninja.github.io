Roles that are given to our offical staff members, each role has different duties.

@Administrators - Leaders and managers of the team

@Moderators - Moderate the server and keep the peace

@Assisstants - Assist users around the server, answer questions, etc.

@Staff- role used for pinging and permissions.

@Artist - People who like to create art or content!

@Coder - People whp have experience with coding!

@Event Participant - those who participate in events.

@Boosters - those who have boosted our server!

These are roles used to indicate how active someone participates in our community.

@Veteran - Only given to those who have more than 20,000 messages and been in the server for more than six months .

@Regular - Only given to thoose who have more than 5,000 messages and been in the server for more than three months.

@Newbie - Given to those who have more than 1,000 messages and been in the server for more than a month.

@Member - Automatically given when you complete verification.
